We, the residents, live amidst the lack of infrastructure and whisper our concerns daily. These whispers have transformed into a symbol of our neighborhood. Whispers find their way into articles, appear on social media, and seep into the lives of everyone living here. The whispers have become something special, defining Nguyễn Duy Trinh Street in a unique way.

By turning these whispers into visual and auditory experiences, “Whispers For HOPE” will guide you on a journey from the past to the present, exploring the essence of the Nguyễn Duy Trinh street - “ROAD OF DEATH”
